About

Nyein Chan Aung

I am a street photographer from Yangon, Burma. I'm 24 years old. Since the military coup in 2021, innocent people have been killed for no reason by the coup army. Three years after the coup, the people are facing the worst disaster, What I can do is I want to express art and the struggles, smiles and beauty of the grassroots as loud as possible...

Most of the children who come from homeless families and manual labor families are disproportionately affected by poverty. Some of them have the freedom to study. Some did not receive it. The main reason is poverty. The cause of poverty is because of authoritarian regimes that rule the country inhumanely through fear. Their main income comes from selling beer cans and liquor bottles. The struggles and smiles of the urban grass roots under dictatorship. These are the truths. So you ignore it.
